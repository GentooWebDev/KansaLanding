About Us

Kansa has developed over the last 38 years from a manufacturer of small handicraft items to a leading importer of stained glass and materials and as a manufacturer and designer of decorative lighting and glass panels.

Makers of glass and metal

We specialise in fine detail and handmade glass and stained glass creations.

Made in England

We have a dedicated team of artisans, crafts people and metal workers, working from our converted flour mill in the heart of Elsecar. We are proud to be one of the largest UK manufacturers of custom and decorative lighting.
